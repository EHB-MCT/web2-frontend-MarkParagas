Chamomile Tea

Benefits:

Chamomile may lower blood pressure slightly. Taking it with drugs for high blood pressure could cause blood pressure to drop too low.

Diabetes medications: Chamomile may lower blood sugar. Taking it with diabetes drugs could raise the risk of hypoglycemia or low blood sugar.

How to make chamomile tea:

The flowers are dried and then infused into hot water.

Ingredients

Flowers (Dried)

Water:

240 ml

Time:

1 - 2 min
